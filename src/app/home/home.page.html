MORPION S2 Welcome to Morpion S2

This is a simple two-player (You and Bot) game where each player takes turns marking a 3x3 grid with their symbol (X or O). The goal is to be the first to get three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal. The game ends in a win for one player or a draw if the grid is fully marked without any player achieving the goal. A game has 10 rounds.
